DYAL SINGH COLLEGE

Dyal Singh College, Karnal, as it stands today is a premier co-educational center of learning of Northern india. With a strengh of 3693 students, the college has all the three streams of learning.

ABOUT





Founder

Our Illustrious Founder
Sardar Dyal Singh Majithia

The institution creates and enhances the existing infrastructure as per the needs coming up from time to time. During the last three years the college has added a new building to the existing science block. This has led to the addition of the state of the art classrooms and laboratories especially for the P.G. courses of science stream. The addition has created more space and rooms for the Master of Arts classes in the old computer science block. These spaces are utilized to conduct weekly seminars for the Post-Graduate students. In addition to it, the college already has a separate block for commerce stream and a separate arts block for Under Graduate classes...
